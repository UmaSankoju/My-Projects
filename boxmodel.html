UI(User Interface) / UX(User Experience) Job Roles


The Role of User Research in UI Design:


User Research plays a critical role in shaping effective User Interfaces (UI). While UI focuses on visual design, layout, and interaction, user research ensures that these decisions are grounded in real user needs and behaviors rather than assumptions.

Role of an Interaction Designer:


An Interaction Designer focuses on how users engage with a product by designing flows, micro-interactions, and feedback responses that make digital experiences smooth, intuitive, and engaging.

Role of an Information Architect:

UX Writer:


A UX Writer is responsible for crafting the words users see and interact with in digital products.They write microcopy like button labels, error messages, tooltips, and onboarding text that guide users smoothly.Their role is to make content clear, concise, and user-friendly, reducing confusion and improving usability.

Visual Designer:


A Visual Designer focuses on the look and feel of a digital product.They work with colors, typography, imagery, icons, and layouts to create a strong visual identity.Their goal is to make the interface not only aesthetically appealing but also consistent with the brand.
